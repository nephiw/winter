Sock Drive

This year the Belle Creek Metro District is working with Henderson Community Church to keep the winter warm for the homeless and struggling in and around our community. Even in a small neighborhood like ours, there are people in need and we want to do our part to help them get back on their feet; so we are collecting and distributing socks! At any time in the month of December, bring new socks to donate to the Family Center and we will get them to women and children in need. There will also be a table to collect socks, and to learn more, at the Community Ugly-Christmas-Sweater Party on December 15th, from 5 - 8 at the Family Center.

If we collect more socks than will be useful within our community, we will be giving them to the Women's Homelessness Initiative and the Tennyson Center for Children to make sure each and every sock we collect will do the most good it can.
